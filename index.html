Why KCBL

Community first. Basketball for every kid in Kailua.

The Kailua Community Basketball League has been building character, teamwork, and skills for youth across Kailua and Windward O'ahu. From first-time players to competitive athletes, every player belongs here.

  • Ko’olau-inspired background and local visual tone
  • Visible sponsor pathway and sponsor tiers
  • Easy online registration and inquiry form
  • Programs for youth ages 6 through high school
  • Family-friendly atmosphere rooted in aloha

Programs

Programs for players, families, and year-round growth

League Season

Co-ed league play with a focus on fundamentals, sportsmanship, fair play, and a great family experience.

Skills Clinics

Spring training opportunities to build confidence, basketball IQ, teamwork, and player development.

Girls Select

More opportunities for girls to compete, improve, and represent the KCBL community.

More than Basketball

More than a league — it's ohana.

We believe youth sports build more than athletic skills. They build confidence, friendships, and community. KCBL is committed to creating a welcoming, family-friendly experience rooted in the spirit of aloha.

“A great league site should make parents feel welcome, players feel excited, and sponsors feel proud to support the program.”
