Why KCBL

A more local, welcoming, and progressive KCBL website

This rebuild keeps the spirit and key imagery of the current KCBL site while presenting it in a cleaner format designed for parents, sponsors, and supporters on phones and desktops.

Instead of an older single-page feel, this version introduces structured sections for programs, community, sponsorship, and registration — making it easier for families to act and easier for sponsors to see value.

What changed

  • Ko‘olau-inspired visual treatment for a stronger Kailua / O‘ahu identity
  • No outdoor court imagery
  • Dedicated sponsor area and sponsor inquiry path
  • Modern call-to-action flow for parents
  • Fast static structure for Cloudflare Pages
